PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

Welcome to PagePulse! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect and how we use, share, and protect it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you use our platform, including:

  • Name and email address for account creation and communication purposes.
  • Content you upload or post on the platform.
  • Usage data such as IP addresses, browser type, and interactions with the site.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services. This includes:

  • Providing access to the platform and its features.
  • Sending account-related notifications and updates.
  • Personalizing your experience on the platform.
  • Improving the performance and functionality of the platform.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders.
  • To protect the rights, property, and safety of our users or PagePulse.
  • To third-party service providers who assist us in delivering the platform's services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure servers. However, no method of data transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also request a copy of your data by contacting us at support@pagepulse.com.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our platform. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the platform may not work properly without them.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will reflect the changes. By continuing to use the platform, you agree to the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@pagepulse.com.

Thank you for trusting PagePulse with your information!
